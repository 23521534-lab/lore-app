# lore. ✦
### *track everything you read, watch & listen to — beautifully.*

---

Lore is a personal media archive with soul. Built for the people who consume too much to remember and feel too much to stay silent — it's the aesthetic alternative to messy Notion databases and soulless spreadsheets.

Log films, books, music, podcasts, YouTube channels, games, and courses. Write reviews. Build moodboards. Share lists with friends. And slowly, Lore builds a portrait of who you are through everything you've ever loved.

---

## what's inside

**✦ home feed** — a visual, scrollable archive of your entire media life. masonry film posters, book shelves, album art. nothing looks the same twice.

**⟡ search & discover** — find anything by title, type, or vibe. see what people with similar taste are logging.

**◎ add anything** — log in seconds. film, book, album, podcast, game, course — with status (finished / watching / want to) and a personal rating.

**⬡ lore boards** — drag-and-drop moodboards made from your own logged items. create by mood, season, or aesthetic. share as a visual post.

**˖° reviews & reflections** — write what you actually thought. not stars. real words. memorable, recommend, revisit — and anything else on your mind.

**ᯓ★ want to list** — a running queue of everything you plan to watch, read, or listen to. never forget a recommendation again.

---

## the aesthetic

dark base with pastel gradients — peach, lilac, sage, blush. editorial fonts. unicode symbols instead of generic emoji. every screen feels like a page from a very well-curated journal.

---

## built with

- React Native + Expo
- Zustand (state)
- Expo Router (navigation)
- Linear Gradient + custom design system

---

## setup

```bash
npm install
npx expo install @expo-google-fonts/syne @expo-google-fonts/space-grotesk
npx expo start
```

scan the QR with Expo Go on your phone, or run `--ios` / `--android` for a simulator.
